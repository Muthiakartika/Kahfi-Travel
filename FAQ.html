Kahfi FAQ

This FAQ provides answer to basic question about Kahfi.

  • Pay for your booking by following these steps:
    1. After completing your booking details, select your preferred payment method.
    2. Tap the Pay with button.
    Please make sure to settle your payment before the time limit expires.
    To check how much time you have left to settle your payment, go to Purchase List.

  • To pay using the Transfer method, follow these steps:
    1. Tap Pay With Transfer after choosing your bank.
    2. Please complete your payment before the time limit expires.
    3. The time limit is located on the Complete Payment page.
    4. Choose Transfer in your bank menu.
    5. Transfer can be done via ATM, mobile banking, or online banking.
    6. Fill in Kahfi account number.
    7. Then, enter the payment amount exactly as stated on your invoice to the last three digits.
    8. Fill your Booking ID in the reference number field on the ATM or online banking/m-banking.

  • To pay for your booking with ATM, follow these steps:
    1. After you’ve completed your booking details, choose ATM on the payment page.
    2. Tap Pay with ATM.
    3. Make sure to enter the correct bank code, Traveloka bank account number,
    4. and exact transaction amount, then proceed with the transfer.
    5. Payment with ATM is available 24/7.

  • To pay for your booking with a credit card, follow these steps:
    1. After you’ve completed your booking details, choose Credit Card on the payment page.
    2. Fill in your credit card details, then tap Pay with Credit Card.
    3. Once your credit card payment is completed, you will receive your e-ticket/voucher on the email address you used for booking within 60 minutes.
