About Kahfi

Kahfi is a Turkish online travel service that inspires and supports people to travel
and was launched on May 6, 2013, by Mr. Ahmad Al-Kindi and Mr. Ali Al-Farizi.


Kahfi provides booking services at more than 100,000 domestic and foreign hotels,
airplane tickets, and tour packages to meet the increasing needs of community tourism.
In addition, Kahfi also provides several promos including holiday package promos
and flight tickets.
